2. Collection, Use, and Processing of Data

Without your explicit consent, we will only this data for providing and performing our services. We will only forward your personal data without your explicit consent to third parties if this is necessary for performing our services or for complying with statutory obligations.

We will save your personal data for providing and performing our services. We will delete your personal data after the end of the contract, if are no longer necessary for invoicing purposes or statutory obligations. If a deletion conflicts with statutory, contractual, or by-law imposed storage periods, the data shall be suspended instead.

2.2 If you are using Learnandroid, we will automatically collect personal usage data that is necessary for performing the services and/or invoicing. Personal data is only collected if you register an account. In accordance with statutory provisions, only data necessary for the respective service is collected.

In particular, these are your email address, your password, your country, and your language. To the extent that we ask for additional information, such information is always optional and marked as such.

We will use your provided data without your separate consent only for fulfilling and performing of our services and for complying with statutory requirements. Forwarding of your personal data to third parties shall only occur if this is necessary for performing our services or for complying with statutory requirements.

Your accruing personal data regarding the access history will be deleted immediately after its termination or, if this is legally forbidden, suspended.
